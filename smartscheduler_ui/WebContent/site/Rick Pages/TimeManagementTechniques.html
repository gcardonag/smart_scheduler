Why time management techniques?
  • 43% of Americans categorize themselves as disorganized and 21% have missed vital work deadlines.1
  • 75% of IT project failures are caused by missed deadlines.2
  • Stress is a common side effect of students with poor time management skills.3
Pomodoro Technique
Study periods are up to 25 minutes, with 3-5 minute intervals between period. After 4 of these periods, 15-30 minute breaks are needed.
Earliest Gap Earliest Deadline first
This consists of placing pomodoros in the earliest available gap in a person's generated schedule, prioritizing those that have the earliest deadline.
